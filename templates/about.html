Skrolla

Skrolla uses your phone's accelerometer to scroll web pages up and down. When you tip the phone towards you, it should scroll up and vice versa. The 'trigger zones' are illustrated below.

Send links to Skrolla for later reading using our Chrome extension.

Unfortunately, any domains with CORS / same-origin restrictions are going to fail (pretty much everything from google & github).

The proof of concept was a fun hack using gyro.js for Git@Me. Later, I incorporated xframe.js to control scrolling in the embedded iframe.

I'm still working on the code and I'd love to get your feedback. Thanks!

Skrolla is built with Flask, Python, Bootstrap, Font Awesome, Firebase, JavaScript, OAuth, jQuery, and very little sleep.
